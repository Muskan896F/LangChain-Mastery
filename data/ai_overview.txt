Artificial Intelligence (AI)

Artificial Intelligence is a branch of computer science focused on building smart machines capable of performing tasks 
that typically require human intelligence. These tasks include problem-solving, learning, reasoning, perception, 
and language understanding.

Key Areas of AI:
1. Machine Learning (ML)
2. Natural Language Processing (NLP)
3. Computer Vision
4. Robotics
5. Expert Systems

Applications of AI:
- Virtual assistants (e.g., Siri, Alexa)
- Autonomous vehicles
- Medical diagnosis
- Fraud detection
- Personalized recommendations

AI continues to revolutionize industries and redefine the future of work and everyday life.
